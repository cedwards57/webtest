Md Mahfuzur Rahman

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Georgia State University. My research interests primarily lie in the shared space of XAI, medical imaging, and computer vision. In my ongoing research, I mainly focus on developing machine learning/deep learning models and leveraging explainable AI to advance our understanding of brain disorders. I put my endeavor into creating new learning algorithms, explanation methods, metrics, and frameworks useful to build interpretable AI models and apply them to broaden our understanding in the neuroscience space.
