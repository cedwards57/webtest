Research Summary

I am interested in the following topics (with selected works):

  • Self-Supervision: Natural Images [ICLR 2019], Neuroimaging [IEEE BHI 2019][MICCAI 2020][NeurIPS ML4H Workshop 2019]
  • Multimodal Learning: Neuroimaging [IEEE ICHI 2021]
  • Out-of-distribution Generalization, Robustness and Fairness: Neuroimaging [ICLR RobustML Workshop 2021]
  • Interpretability: Neuroimaging [IEEE ISBI 2021], [Preprint]
  • Image Segmentation: Brain Tissue [IJCNN 2017], Atlas [BigNeuro Workshop NeurIPS 2017]

I also worked on Reinforcement Learning [NeurIPS Deep RL Workshop 2015] and Computer Vision problems (traffic sign recognition; age and gender recognition from human faces).
